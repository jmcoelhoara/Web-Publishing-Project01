Turbo
Compresses exhaust gas as it exits and
sends it back into the engine. The increased airflow sends more
fuel into the engine, giving it more power.
Throttle Body
Controll the amount of air that flows into your car's engine
Alternador
Convert chemical energy to electrical energy so that you
can charge and replenish the battery in your engine and other electrical components in a car.
Piston
Transfer force from expanding gas in the cylinder to
the crankshaft via a piston rod and/or connecting rod.
